How it works

Under the hood

WCF Scout uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) with the full WCF 2026 scoring rulebook embedded in every analysis.

STEP 01
📋

You enter the details

Player name, position, ownership %, fixture, and recent form. The more context you give, the sharper the analysis.

STEP 02
🤖

Claude analyses

The AI references official WCF scoring rules to calculate a realistic point ceiling, broken down action by action.

STEP 03
⚡

Scouting bonus check

If the player is under 5% owned, the +2 scouting bonus is automatically factored into the ceiling score.

STEP 04
✅

Verdict delivered

Strong Pick / Moderate / Risky verdict with key risks, captaincy potential, and a one-line final call.
